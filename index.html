Introduction

As part of my service learning experience, I served at Mark's Adventures Day Care, beginning October 1st, 2024. This opportunity allowed me to work with young children, helping them develop their creative and social skills through various activities and projects.

I chose this service site because of my passion for early childhood education and my desire to make a positive impact on children's development during their formative years. The experience has been incredibly rewarding, allowing me to witness firsthand the joy of discovery and learning in young minds.

Daily Activities and Impact
