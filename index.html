Introduction

As part of my service learning experience, I served at Mark's Adventures Day Care, beginning October 1st, 2024. This opportunity allowed me to work with young children, helping them develop their creative and social skills through various activities and projects.

I selected this service site because of my interest in enhancing the learning of the children at a tender age when their developmental status is determinable. It has been thoroughly rewarding to be a part of this whole process and observe the learning process going on in the kids.

From this activity, I hoped to gain substantial skills in communication, problem solving, working as a team, and the opportunity to contribute to the community.

Reflections

Growth and Learning

By working at Mark’s Adventures Day Care, I’ve grown not only as an employee and as a childcare professional. I have understood the need to communicate with the young children in a manner that fosters learning and also nurture their creativity Citation.

Challenges Faced and Successes

There were also some difficulties which I met working in the day care, for example, it was quite difficult for me to be able to handle along with several kids at the same time.

Nevertheless, I managed to surmount these challenges by being disciplined, acting on them proactively and lastly by involving the staff. What I was self-satisfied with was the numerous activities I managed to perform with the children, as well as their ability to do crafts and any other creative activity that was needed.

Through the use of this service-learning I have been able to advance both personally and professionally. I have come to appreciate the need to be very patient, be compassionate and reasonable while dealing with young children. In the same regard, the knowledge of the typical running of an early childhood education center has helped me develop my desire to work in this institution more keenly.

Daily Activities and Impact

Throughout my service, I engaged in various creative activities with the children, including:

  • Seasonal art projects (Halloween pumpkin drawings, handprint crafts)
  • Creative expression through drawing and coloring
  • Interactive group activities promoting social skills
  • Supporting individual creative development
  • Organizing and facilitating group art sessions
